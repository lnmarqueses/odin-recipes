Adobo Recipe

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo. It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are version wherein onions are also added. Adobo is a popular dish in the Philippines, along with Sinigang.

  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 2 tbs garlic
  • 5 pcs bay leaves
  • 4tbs vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
    • <1i>tablespoon peppercorn
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Combine the pork belly, soy sauce, and garlic then marinade for at least 1 hour
  • Heat the pot and put-in the marinated pork belly then cook for a few minutes
  • Pour remaining marinade including garlic.
  • Add water, whole pepper corn, and dried bay leaves then bring to a boil.
  • Simmer for 40 minutes to 1 hour
  • Put-in the vinegar and simmer for 12 to 15 minutes
  • Add salt to taste
  • Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
